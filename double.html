Double Dragons

Double Element Dragons are powerful hybrids, blending the might of two forces. Once neutral in combat, they now thrive with unique strengths and vulnerabilities as new elements arise. With their dual nature, they strike with versatility, but careful strategy is needed to unlock their true power in battle!

Double Terra Dragon

Don't be afraid! That's not an earthquake, just a dignified Double Terra Dragon out for a walk. These guys aren't known for their beauty, but rather for their love of dirt and their extremely humble and honest nature.

Double Flame Dragon

If you can't take the heat, stay away from the Double Flame Dragon! This temperamental creature is easily set off but calms down just as fast. And he always feels deep remorse for the things he burned.

Double Sea Dragon

If you're into watersports, the Double Sea Dragon will be your new best friend. They are often mistaken for seals or dolphins...

Double Nature Dragon

The Nature Dragon loves humans, animals and all living things, some of them for gastronomic purposes. Even though never known to eat whole human being, a finger or two have been lost...

Double Electricitic Dragon

The tale tells us this dragon was born in a great storm that took place in the dark ages when an ordinary dragon got hit by a lightning. Since that day, you can see them on the skies, looking for a shock.

Double Ice Dragon

With the recent climate changes, the super cool Ice Dragons have migrated to the North Pole. If you see one in a warm place, please direct them to the nearest ice rink and you'll have a friend forever.

Double Metal Dragon

A robust dragon, drawn towards all things of metal. The word on the island is that what the Metallic Dragon lacks in intellect, it more than makes up in determination.

Double Primal Dragon

Although the Double Primal Dragon is an agile, attacking beast, this ferocious dragon is loyal and has a soft heart. His pointed canines are both a benefit and a drawback as they often get in the way when he wants to play around with his friends.

Double Pure Dragon

The Double Pure Dragon is not as innocent as he looks. He often gets mistaken for a cloud and he goes with it. This sneaky dragon is known to create “bad weather” to distract his enemies and likes to float over other habitats to hear all the gossip
