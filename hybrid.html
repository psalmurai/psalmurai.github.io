Hybrid Dragons

Hybrid Dragons (Hybrids) are Dragons possessing more than one Element. The first Element of a Dragon is called the Primary/Defending Element. The Primary/Defending Element determines its type (e.g., the Flaming Rock Dragon is called a Terra Hybrid) and is used for damage calculation in Battles. The remaining Elements (Secondary Elements) of a Dragon determines what type of attacks it is capable of, and what type of attacks it can be trained in.

Tropical Dragon

Terra + Nature. This dragon is breedable for 7 hours. It can be obtained by breeding Terra Dragon and Nature Dragon. This may also turn into other dragons with the Terra and Nature typing

Nenufar Dragon

Nature + Sea. Nenúfar is the Spanish term for a female water lily, which has larger pistils but smaller stamens than the male. Nenufar Dragon looks similar to the Pokémon Bulbasaur and its evolutions, Ivysaur and Venusaur.

Cloud Dragon

Sea + Nature. A cloud is a visible mass of liquid droplets or frozen crystals made of water or various chemicals suspended in the atmosphere above the surface of a planetary body. Cloud Dragon has the same body shape than Storm Dragon.

Laser Dragon

Flame + Electric. This futuristic dragon is a delightful addition to your islands. It's highly energetic and likes to fry small insects with its laser beams. This means you can also have bug control on your islands!

Fluorescent Dragon

Electric + Ice. This one glows in the dark, so it's highly convenient during the dark winter months. You will also save in electricity by getting this glowy Dragon!

Pearl Dragon

Ice + Metal. The Pearl Dragon is known to be a bit vain and often wastes considerable amounts of time in shining her pearls. But when needed, she can be extremely brave and selfless and helps the ones in need.

Rainbow Dragon

Metal + Light. This dragon takes white light and turns it into the whole light spectrum, coating herself with all the colors you can see - and probably some that you can't!

Elfic Dragon

Light + War. Since the first time the Elves walked over Arda this dragon has been protecting them all. His armor is made of leather strips, iron ingot and refined moonstones. And he is immune to all the forces of evil.

Dark Stone Dragon

Dark + War. Like volcanic rock he's extra lightweight so he is able to fly with those small wings at sound speed. Those thick arms and horns seems to deals super damage but being so porous his punches are harmless.

Dual Blade Dragon

This creature was born ready for war: He has blades instead of horns! This peculiarity made him quite dangerous when he was just a clumsy baby, but as soon as he gained full control of his movements and his strength, he became the unstoppable dragon he is today!.

Equinox Dragon

The Equinox Dragon has a theory: everyone has a dark side and a light side. The secret to happiness is balancing these two in the same way the equinox perfectly balances day and night.
