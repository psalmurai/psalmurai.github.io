Island

In the enchanted world of Dragon City, vast floating islands serve as sanctuaries for dragons. Shaped by ancient magic, these islands come in varied forms, making it impossible to perfectly use all the space. Early on, players build small Habitats, each taking 4x4 spaces, but as their power grows, they unlock larger 6x6 Habitats to house their mighty dragons. As the number of dragons increased, the original islands could no longer contain them all.

In response, the magic of Dragon City expanded, revealing the hidden Desert Island and Skull Island to the bravest players. As the skies continued to shift, more islands appeared—Rainbow Island, with its radiant beauty, Ice Island, frozen in eternal winter, and the mysterious Gothic Island, shrouded in shadow. These mystical realms were unlocked only when all previous lands were claimed. In time, the cosmic forces of the game unveiled Rune Island, Future Island, and the celestial Moon Island. With these new lands, the number of dragon habitats grew, allowing for even greater collections of rare and powerful creatures.

To celebrate the 10th anniversary of Dragon City, three legendary islands emerged from the mist—Tempest Island, swirling with stormy skies, Jurassic Island, teeming with ancient power, and Chronos Island, a realm where time itself bends. Each new island holds untold secrets, waiting to be discovered by those brave enough to master them!

Main Island

The heart of Dragon City, the Main Island is where your dragon journey begins. It’s home to essential structures like the Hatchery and Breeding Mountain, serving as the foundation for your growing dragon empire.

Lush Island

A vibrant paradise in Dragon City, Lush Island is filled with fertile grounds and endless greenery. It’s the perfect haven for Nature and Earth dragons, offering abundant space to grow and thrive in a tranquil, magical environment...

Lava Island

A fiery realm of molten rock and blazing heat, Lava Island is home to Flame and Metal dragons. With its intense energy and volcanic landscape, it's a place where only the toughest dragons thrive, harnessing the power of fire and molten fury.

Ivory Island

A radiant, pristine island bathed in light, Ivory Island is the sanctuary for Light and Pure dragons. Its gleaming white cliffs and tranquil aura make it a place of serenity and power, where dragons of purity and grace gather to shine brighter than ever.

Desert Island

A vast, sun-scorched land, Desert Island is a harsh yet beautiful sanctuary for Earth and Flame dragons. Its golden sands and towering dunes hide ancient secrets, where only the resilient can thrive under the blazing sun and shifting sands.

Rainbow Island

A dazzling island of vibrant colors and shimmering skies, Rainbow Island is a magical realm where dragons of all elements unite. Arcs of light stretch across the horizon, and the air is filled with enchantment, making it a perfect sanctuary for rare and majestic dragons.

Ice Island

A frozen wonderland, Ice Island is a realm of endless winter, home to Ice and Water dragons. Its icy peaks and frosty plains shimmer under the cold light, creating a tranquil yet powerful domain where only the boldest dragons can flourish in the biting chill.

Premium Island

Wonder Island

A realm of awe and enchantment, Wonder Island brims with magical landscapes and fantastical creatures. Here, dragons of all elements thrive, basking in the island’s endless wonders and mysteries that captivate the imagination.

Marvel Islandn

Marvel Island is a dazzling world filled with extraordinary sights and incredible dragons. Its vibrant lands are home to rare and powerful hybrids, where every corner holds a new, marvelous discovery waiting to be uncovered.

Mystic Island

Shrouded in mist and ancient magic, Mystic Island is a realm of secrets and enchantment. It’s where dragons with mystical powers gather, drawing from the island’s deep well of arcane energy and timeless wisdom.

Infinite Island

If the Primal Dragon seems a little nervous, give him some time. He's been looking for Dragon City for a while, but he kept getting distracted between portals. As one of the most playful dragons of the primal element, attention will make him happy.

Lustrous Island

A gleaming paradise of shimmering light and brilliance, Lustrous Island is home to Pure and Light dragons. Its radiant beauty reflects the purity of its inhabitants, where the air sparkles with an otherworldly glow that energizes all who dwell here.
