Super Dragons

Super Dragons in Dragon City are legendary hybrids, known for their extraordinary power and elemental mastery. These dragons combine rare elements, making them stronger and more versatile than most hybrids. They excel in battles, with their supercharged attacks dealing massive damage across a wide range of opponents. Super Dragons are not only harder to obtain but also possess unique abilities, often holding the key to victory in tough battles. Their power makes them the elite of Dragon City, feared and admired by all!.
