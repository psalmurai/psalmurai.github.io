Zombie Dragons

Emerging from the shadows of Dragon City, Zombie Dragons are eerie creatures born from ancient curses and dark magic. With their tattered wings and glowing eyes, these undead dragons embody the essence of the afterlife. They possess unique abilities that allow them to drain energy from their foes, spreading fear and chaos in battles. These fearsome dragons are a haunting reminder of the power of darkness and the mysteries that lie beyond.

Zombie Dragon

This undead dragon has a huge appetite! It keeps eating, but somehow nothing seems to hold in. Despite its constant hunger, forgetful nature and bad vision, it has proven to be loyal.

Zombie Terra Dragon

Unaware of its actions, the Zombie Terra Dragon is usually found in packs, under the control of the Zombie Star Dragon. Terra Dragons scaper at the sight of a zombie hoard, knowing full well that if they get too close, they'll become what they most fear.

Zombie Flame Dragon

The Zombie Flame Dragon used to be a playful Flame Dragon, now it roams Habitats looking for unsuspecting victims. Its body has become at one with its flames, making it unextinguishable. This is a dragon you don't want to come across at night.

Zombie Nature Dragon

The Zombie Nature Dragon has become a common figure among younger dragons' nightmares. Some don't believe in this poisonous foe, but others claim to have witnessed whole forests turn black and lakes turn toxic in this dragon's presence.

Zombie Star Dragon

How do you battle something that is already dead? The Zombie Star Dragon, a close, yet much darker relative of the Star Dragon, is the leader of the undead. By leading over the other zombie dragons, it can send a hoard of nightmares your way.

High Diabolical Dragon

The Scientist and Mad Scientist Dragon dragons see him as a god. The High Diabolical Dragon is a nefarious creature who came into contact with a living, radioactive organism. This created a symbiotic bond, giving him unlimited power and altering his personality...for the worse.
